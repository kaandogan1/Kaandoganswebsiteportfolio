Flow – Brand Identity & Campaign Design

Flow is a brand identity and promotional campaign project centred around a reusable water bottle designed to promote sustainable hydration. The project explores how branding, product design, and marketing visuals can work together to influence consumer behaviour and encourage environmentally friendly habits.

Year

2026

Role

Brand Designer & Visual Creator (Individual Project)

The Challenge

The challenge was to create a strong and visually engaging brand identity that communicates sustainability while still appealing to a modern audience. The brand needed to stand out in a competitive market and clearly express its purpose through both product design and promotional materials.

The Solution

To address this, I developed a cohesive brand identity using a clean and modern visual style. I designed the logo, selected a consistent colour palette, and created typography that reflects simplicity and clarity. In addition to the branding, I produced a range of promotional materials, including posters, billboards, and a 3D product visualisation. These elements were designed to work together as part of a 360° campaign, ensuring consistency across different platforms.

The Outcome

The final outcome is a cohesive brand identity and campaign that effectively communicates the message of sustainability. The project demonstrates how visual design can influence user perception and behaviour through consistent branding and strong visual communication. It highlights my ability to apply design principles across multiple formats, from product design to large-scale promotional media.
