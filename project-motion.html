Glove-Iq

GloveIQ is a smart boxing glove concept designed to enhance athlete performance through real-time data tracking. The project explores how wearable technology can be integrated into sports equipment to provide insights into speed, impact, and technique, helping users improve their training and overall performance.

Year

2024-2025

Role

Product Designer & Concept Developer (Individual Project)

The Challenge

The challenge was to design an innovative boxing glove that goes beyond traditional equipment by incorporating technology to track performance. The product needed to be functional, comfortable, and suitable for training, while also providing meaningful data to help users improve their skills.

The Solution

To address this, I developed GloveIQ as a smart wearable concept that integrates performance-tracking features into a boxing glove. The design focuses on collecting data such as punch speed, force, and movement, allowing users to gain insights into their performance. The glove was designed with both usability and practicality in mind, ensuring it remains comfortable while incorporating advanced functionality.
